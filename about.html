The Heart Behind the Blooms

Hello, I’m Clare!


I started Clare's Creations out of a deep-rooted love for floral design and interior aesthetics. Over time, I realized that fresh flowers, while beautiful, are fleeting.


I wanted to capture that exact moment of full bloom and preserve it forever for cosy modern homes. Thus, my journey into high-quality, ultra-realistic faux floristry began.


Every single arrangement is a labor of love. From selecting the perfect silk petals to finding the right, rustic, or minimalist modern vase, I pour my imagination into creating pieces that are handmade, heartfelt, and home ready.


Thank you deeply for supporting my small business.
